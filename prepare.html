Home Needs

  • Keep these records in a waterproof, portable container:
    Will, insurance policies, contracts, deeds, stocks and bonds, Passports, social security cards, immunization records, Bank account numbers, Credit card account numbers and companies
  • Inventory of valuable household goods, important telephone numbers
  • Family records (birth, marriage, death certificates)
  • Keep items in airtight plastic bags
  • Store your kit in a convenient place known to all family members. Keep a smaller version of the supplies kit in the trunk of your car.
  • Re-think your kit and family needs at least once a year. Replace batteries, update clothes, etc.
  • Change your stored water supply every six months so it stays fresh
  • Replace your stored food every six months
  • Ask your physician or pharmacist about storing prescription medications

Protect and Reinforcing your Home

  • Make sure your home can be secured from water, checking doors, garage doors, roof, and windows
  • Board up windows
  • Hurricane shutters
  • Secure outdoor items
  • Prepare for power outages
  • Identify your home with legible and clearly marked street name and house number so emergency vehicles can rapidly find the location of the emergency.

Know your Insurance and What it Covers

Check what coverage is on things like:

  • Water damage
  • Lightning strikes
  • Vandalism/malicious mischief
  • Theft
  • Falling Objects
