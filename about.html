About Us



We're an interdisciplinary trio at the University of Texas at Austin looking for ways to spread awareness for hurricane preparedness.


After working with many different technologies over our college career, for our senior project we wanted to look at ways to help people in our community with the skills that we've learned. With us all living in Texas and experiencing hurricanes all the time, we tend to get complacent with them and either not evacuate or properly prepare for them. So we wanted to help ourselves and other people relearn and remind them of the importance of hurricane preparedness. We did that in two parts: 1) this website that's filled with educational information and 2) a virtual reality experience that takes a look at the destructive properties of hurricanes. With this website, we found that while there are tons with websites with amazing information on hurricanes, they tend to be overwhelming and confusing to navigate. We wanted to create something that a person with little knowledge on the subject can explore and learn the basics without having to dig. We wanted to split up the site into how to prepare, endure, and recover from hurricanes because we found that many websites focused solely on preparedness and rarely talk about what to do after. Information is always changing so this website will never be fully final but we would love to hear from you about our project. Thanks for visiting!
