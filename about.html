About

We're a trio at the University of Texas at Austin working to spread awareness!


This is our senior project looking at ways to help people be more prepared for hurricanes. Living in Texas and experiencing hurricanes all the time, we could get complacent with them and not evacuate or properly prepare. So we wanted to help people relearn the importance of hurricane preparedness and we did that in two parts: this website that's filled with educational information and a virtual reality experience that takes a look at the destructive properties of hurricanes. Feel free to give us feedback on our project!
